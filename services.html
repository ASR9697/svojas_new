Comprehensive Transportation Solutions

At Svojas Inc., we offer a full suite of logistics services designed to handle any shipping challenge. Our expertise ensures your freight is managed safely, efficiently, and cost-effectively. Explore our services below to find the right solution for your business.

Full Truckload (FTL)

When you have enough freight to fill an entire trailer, our FTL service is your most efficient option. We provide a dedicated truck for your shipment, ensuring it goes directly from origin to destination without any intermediate stops. This minimizes handling, reduces transit time, and provides maximum security for your cargo.

  • Faster transit times for time-sensitive deliveries.
  • Exclusive use of trailer space for your goods only.
  • Less handling, significantly reducing the risk of damage.
  • Ideal for high-value, large, or delicate shipments.

Less-than-Truckload (LTL)

For shipments that don't require a full truck, our LTL service is the perfect solution. We consolidate your freight with other shipments heading in the same direction, allowing you to share the cost of transportation. It's a flexible and budget-friendly option for smaller loads without sacrificing reliability.

  • Significant cost savings by paying only for the space you use.
  • Access to a vast network of destinations across the country.
  • Perfect for small businesses and recurring smaller shipments.

Drayage Services

Drayage is the critical first and last link in the intermodal shipping chain. Our expert team manages the movement of your containers from ports and rail terminals to their next destination, whether it's a warehouse, distribution center, or another transport hub. We handle the complex coordination to prevent delays and costly demurrage fees.

  • Service to all major US ports and rail ramps.
  • Timely pickups and drop-offs to meet tight schedules.
  • Coordination with steamship lines and railways for smooth transfers.

Air Freight Logistics

When speed is your top priority, our air freight logistics services provide the ground support you need. We coordinate the expedited pickup and delivery of your air cargo, ensuring it gets to and from the airport on schedule. We manage the crucial ground link for both domestic and international air shipments.

  • Time-sensitive and expedited delivery management.
  • Seamless coordination with air carriers and ground handlers.
  • Secure handling of high-value and urgent goods.

Specialized & OOG Cargo

Shipping oversized or unconventional freight requires meticulous planning and specialized equipment. Our Out of Gauge (OOG) and specialized cargo team has the expertise to handle your most challenging loads, including heavy machinery, industrial components, and construction equipment. We manage everything from route planning and permitting to securement and final delivery.

  • Heavy haul and oversized load management.
  • Access to specialized equipment like flatbeds, step-decks, and RGN trailers.
  • Full coordination of necessary permits and escorts.
